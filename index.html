About Me


I am a web developer transitioning from a professional career in classical music. I worked as a solo horn player with one of the oldest symphonic orchestras in Europe, where discipline, precision, and long-term focus were part of everyday work.


Music has been a natural part of my life from an early age. I was led to it by my father, and thanks to him, I developed a deep respect for craft, consistency, and quiet excellence. These values continue to shape the way I approach my work today.


Due to health-related reasons, I made a conscious decision to transition from the world of professional performance to software development. This shift was not an escape, but a deliberate move toward a field where I can build, think, and grow sustainably over the long term.


In web development, I focus on clarity, maintainability, and thoughtful structure. I enjoy building things that make sense — technically, logically, and humanly.


My professional motto is "Quality is fancy."
My personal motto is "Everything bad can be good for something new."


I am currently based in Prague, Czech Republic, and open to remote work, relocation, and collaboration across borders.


Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Next.js, Django.
